Under Settings > Working Time & Scheduling > Scheduling Basics you set the company-wide default values for vacation, contract durations, surcharges, and the visibility of the schedule.

## How it works

**Vacation and deadlines:** Vacation days per year, probation period (in months), and notice period (in days) as plain numeric fields.

**Payment interval:** Choice between Weekly, Biweekly, Monthly, and Custom.

**Surcharges:** Night, holiday, Saturday, and Sunday surcharge, each in percent; entries are automatically clamped to the 0–100 range.

**Visibility in the schedule:** "Days before" and "days after" determine how many days before and after the current date are visible in the schedule (0–31 days, also automatically clamped).

**Saving:** This card lives in the Working Time & Scheduling section of the settings page (alongside Scheduling Rules and Wizard Settings). Changes are collected and saved or discarded via the settings page's shared save bar — there is no dedicated save button on this card.

## Good to know

- These values are company-wide **default values**. Each individual contract template can additionally have its own, independent surcharge rates, its own payment interval, and its own holiday calendar (see Settings > Contracts) — see that page for details on how the two levels interact.
- The surcharge rates should be understood as percentages/multipliers, not as absolute amounts.
