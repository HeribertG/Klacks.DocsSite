# Klacksy: Your AI Assistant for Scheduling

"Fill next week's early shifts" — that one sentence is all it takes. Klacksy is the built-in AI assistant that operates Klacks through natural language, typed or spoken.

## How it works

Klacksy understands what you want and translates it into concrete actions — with more than **170 capabilities** at the ready:

- **Scheduling:** fill shifts, replace no-shows, rebook assignments
- **Managing:** create employees, maintain groups, set permissions
- **Navigating:** "Show me the schedule for Team Bern" opens the right view
- **Explaining:** Klacksy answers questions about the app and guides new users through the setup

Before every change, Klacksy summarizes what is about to happen and waits for your confirmation — nothing happens behind your back.

## Free choice of model — even fully local

Here's what sets it apart: **you choose the language model yourself.** OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, DeepSeek, and other providers are supported — or a locally hosted model. That enables the combination hardly any vendor can offer: an AI assistant where **not a single personal record ever leaves the building**. Exactly what hospitals, home-care services, and security firms need.

## Good to know

- Klacksy speaks the four core languages (German, French, Italian, English) and, via language plugins, 21 more.
- Through a built-in Model Context Protocol server, Klacks can also be connected to external AI tools.
- Klacksy is live in the Playground — just ask it something.
